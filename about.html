About Scholarly

Connecting Readers
to Knowledge Since 1998

Scholarly is an independent academic publishing and library platform dedicated to making peer-reviewed books accessible to students, researchers, and lifelong learners worldwide.

We partner with over 200 universities and research institutions, maintaining a growing collection of more than 48,000 titles across all major disciplines.

Open Access

We believe knowledge should be free. Over 30% of our collection is fully open-access — available to anyone, anywhere, with no subscription required.

🔬

Peer-Reviewed Quality

Every title in our collection undergoes a rigorous editorial review process. We maintain the highest standards for accuracy and scholarly integrity.
